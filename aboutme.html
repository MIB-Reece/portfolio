Maurice Onyeani

Software Engineer

How it all started

Since primary school my parents enrolled me in extra curricular activities regarding computer science; I was obsessed with video games and attempted to reverse engineer some of the things I enjoyed.

The following years

I gained interest in computer programming when I was in middle school I learned a multitude of basic business skills and became comfortable with office work; my family always had a home office thanks to my dad. In highschool I became fluent with HTML5, stronger with javascript and proficient in Web Design thanks to my enthusiastic teachers that gave me a fun learning journey. History and Computer Science were always my favorite school subjects.

The start of my professional work

As I grew up I lost interest in being a game developer. I gained a new found appreciation for people with the determination to create the entertainment that we take for granted. I found it easier to expand my software engineering skills by building multiple websites for online businesses. my E-commerce experience gave me a new perspective on what companies need to dominate the market as more sales move online.
